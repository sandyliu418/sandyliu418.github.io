Sandy Liu

Sandy is a high school student at Ruth Asawa School of the Arts. She has been involved in music performances throughout the Bay Area including Mudic Teachers' Assoication of California Festival and Philharmonia Baroque Masterclass. In 2015, she was nominated amongst her school to audition for the Community Music Center's Young Musicians Program. As of 2021, she has been a High School Music Teaching Intern for the Middle School Ensemble.

She looks forward to applying her various art forms to develop and produce an Indie Game.
